## 🌸 PROJECT OVERVIEW

Bloom Bouquet Platform is a modern floral e-commerce application focused on personalized bouquet shopping, floral recommendations, AI-assisted styling, and seamless online ordering.

The platform allows users to:
- Explore bouquet collections
- View bouquet details
- Add bouquets to cart
- Register and login
- Place floral orders
- Use AI Stylist recommendations
- Use voice bouquet ordering
